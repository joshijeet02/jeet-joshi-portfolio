Grant Intelligence System

Deployed

For a tribal rights CSO managing multi-crore grants from international foundations

Grant writing was entirely manual — funders identified one by one, proposals rebuilt from scratch every cycle. The system scouts live funding opportunities, profiles each funder in depth, evaluates organizational fit with a GO/NO-GO score, generates proposal concepts, and exports formatted Word documents. Built with a RAG pipeline indexed against the organization's real grant history. Running in production.

VAAGDHARA — Voice Agent & Knowledge Base

Pilot-Ready

Hindi-first voice AI for tribal women (Bhil community) in Rajasthan, MP & Gujarat

Frontline workers needed to have empathetic, culturally grounded conversations with tribal women about their rights, livelihoods, and government schemes. The knowledge lived in PDFs and newsletters nobody could use in the field. Built a structured Hindi knowledge base from primary sources, a 200-question scored dialogue bank, and a pilot-ready voice agent system prompt. Designed for voice platforms; includes an OCR pipeline for Hindi newsletters.

Voice AI Hindi NLP Community Development Human-Centered AI

Rajasthan Employment Dashboard

Deployed

Built for a government policy briefing — 15 years of MNREGA data across 33 districts

MNREGA generates massive data. Most of it sits on government MIS servers, inconsistently formatted, impossible to compare across years or political tenures. Built a full scraping and normalization pipeline, then a React + TypeScript dashboard designed for one question: did employment actually improve, or just increase? The distinction matters — high persondays can mean strong implementation or severe drought. Includes tenure comparison, district drill-down, finance layers, and a methodology section.

Public Policy Government Data Data Engineering Economic Analysis React

Swaraj Loom — Knowledge Platform

Deployed

Learning management system for indigenous ecological knowledge — 11 partner organizations

Eleven partner organizations had spent decades documenting indigenous ecological knowledge. It lived in reports and conversations — no one could navigate it. Built a full-stack LMS with a 3D content matrix (5 natural elements × 3 time dimensions × 6 lenses), a 4-level learning progression, community contribution with moderation, full-text search, and a partner ecosystem portal. Firebase backend, Netlify deployed, editorial-quality content.

LMS Knowledge Systems Firebase NGO Indigenous Knowledge

Sustainability Intelligence — Norex Flavours

In Discussion

The only Indian MSME in the fragrance sector with EcoVadis Gold — 5,000 mint farmers in Western UP

Norex is moving to the EcoVadis large company category next year, which requires living income disclosure for all farmers they work with. UP state government has not even calculated a living income benchmark. Designed three systems: a carbon Scope 1/2/3 calculator with auto-updating emission factors, a living income methodology built from household expenditure data for UP farmers, and a digitized farm dashboard to replace paper diaries used in a Swiss regenerative pilot.

Agricultural Economics EcoVadis Living Income Sustainability

How I work

01
Economics first. Every problem has a structure — who bears the cost, what information is missing, what incentive is pulling the wrong way. I find that structure before building anything.
02
Build for the specific situation. Not a general version of the problem — this organization, this data, this team, these constraints. Generic tools solve generic problems.
03
Deploy. Leave it running. No reports that sit. No demos that get pitched and shelved. Systems that actually work, in production, for real people.
