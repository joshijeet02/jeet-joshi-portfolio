Work Writing About Talk to me

Tell me what you are working on.

I will tell you honestly whether I can help.

If I can, I will explain how I would approach it and what it would take to build. If I cannot, I will tell you that too — and probably point you toward someone who can.

I respond personally. Usually within a day.

jeetjoshieconomics@gmail.com

If you prefer WhatsApp, mention it and I will send you my number.

What to include

Tell me what the problem is. What it is costing — in time, money, missed opportunity, or organizational stress. What you have tried. What you are hoping to change.

You do not need to pitch me. You do not need to explain AI or economics. Just describe the situation as clearly as you can. The rest of the conversation will figure itself out.
