FGI Industry Intelligence Dashboard

A Python-scraped intelligence tool mapping the Federation of Gujarat Industries membership — sector composition, chairman cohort, company signals

DeliveredPythonWeb ScrapingBusiness IntelligencePlaywright

What It Does

The Federation of Gujarat Industries (FGI) is one of Gujarat's major industry associations. Their membership directory — 126+ companies — is publicly available but static: a list of names with no sector analysis, no financial signals, no way to prioritize for outreach or research.

This tool turns that directory into an intelligence layer. A Playwright-based scraper pulls the membership page, parses company names and website links, and runs enrichment: website signal extraction (turnover, headcount, export mentions), NSE/BSE listed company matching, and annual report PDF extraction for financial data. The output is a static dashboard showing sector composition, top companies by signals, and a chairman cohort enrichment for the 12 largest members.

Why It Exists

Before meeting with any industry body or pitching to its members, understanding the composition of the membership saves hours of manual research. This dashboard answers: which sectors are overrepresented, which companies have international exposure, which are publicly listed, and which chairman cohort to prioritize for a pilot engagement. The same architecture was used for the VIA (Vatva Industries Association) audit tool, which added an environmental compliance layer from NGT judgment records.
