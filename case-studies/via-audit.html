VIA Compliance Audit Tool

A compliance intelligence tool for Vatva Industries Association — cross-referencing 126 member companies with NGT judgments and environmental records

DeliveredPythonPlaywrightRegulatory DataEnvironmental Economics

What It Does

Vatva, on the outskirts of Ahmedabad, is one of India's densest industrial clusters — chemicals, textiles, pharmaceuticals. The Vatva Industries Association (VIA) represents 600+ member companies. Environmental compliance in this cluster is opaque: NGT judgments, pollution board notices, and legal cases exist in scattered public records that no one has systematically mapped to the company directory.

This tool does that mapping. A Playwright scraper fetches the full VIA directory. A separate pipeline downloads the National Green Tribunal judgment index and runs fuzzy company name matching to identify which VIA members have active NGT cases. Additional layers extract trade portal signals (turnover, workforce, export markets) and annual report financial data. The result is a dashboard that shows which companies have environmental legal exposure — useful for NGO research, policy advocacy, or responsible investment screening.

The Economic Framing

The value of this tool is not in the data engineering. It is in the question: what does it cost when environmental compliance is invisible? For communities near Vatva, the cost is borne in health outcomes and land contamination. For companies with hidden NGT exposure, the cost shows up in investor risk and buyer due diligence. Making that cost visible is what the tool is actually for.
