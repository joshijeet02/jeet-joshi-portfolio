About

I grew up in Banswara, Rajasthan. I trained at the Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics and the University of Sydney. I spent years in policy research — government budgets, rural employment, economic rights — before I started building the systems I kept wishing existed.

I write a newsletter called Chai with an Economist. About 3,000 people read it. I write when something has been sitting with me long enough that not writing it feels wrong.
