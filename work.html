Grant Intelligence System

For a tribal rights CSO managing multi-crore grants from major international foundations

Grant writing was entirely manual. Identifying funders, building proposals, staying aligned with each funder's language and priorities — all of it done person by person. I built a system that scouts opportunities, profiles funders, evaluates fit, generates proposal concepts, and exports formatted drafts. Runs in production.

Rajasthan Employment Dashboard

Built for a government policy briefing — 15 years of employment data across 33 districts

MNREGA data sits on government servers, inconsistently formatted, impossible to compare across years. I built a data pipeline and React TypeScript dashboard that pulls, normalizes, and visualizes the full employment history of Rajasthan. The dashboard is designed for one question: did employment improve, or just increase?

Public Policy Data Engineering Economic Analysis React TypeScript

Swaraj Loom — Knowledge Platform

A learning platform for indigenous ecological knowledge, built for and with a tribal rights NGO

Eleven partner organizations had spent decades documenting indigenous ecological knowledge across India. It lived in reports, newsletters, and conversations. Swaraj Loom organizes it across five natural elements, three time dimensions, and six ways of seeing — with a four-level learning path, community contribution, and a moderation system.

The Coach — AI Executive Coaching App

A coaching product with a subscription model, iOS architecture, and research-backed intelligence

I built the backend for a coaching application that learns who you are in the first eight questions and builds every subsequent session around your actual goals, relationships, and stressors. Conversations are encrypted. Retention-controlled. The premium tier model is designed for iOS monetization.
